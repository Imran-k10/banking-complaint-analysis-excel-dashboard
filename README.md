# Banking Complaint Analysis Dashboard (Excel)

An interactive Excel dashboard analyzing 1,200+ customer complaint records for a banking dataset — built to track resolution performance, identify bottlenecks, and support data-driven service improvements.

📌 Built during my Data Analyst Internship at **Blue Kwet Consulting**.

## 🎯 Objective

Banks handle a high volume of customer complaints across multiple channels, and resolution delays directly impact customer trust. This dashboard was built to answer:

- What's the overall complaint resolution rate and average resolution time?
- Which complaint types take the longest to resolve?
- How is complaint volume distributed across service channels?
- How satisfied are customers with the resolution process?

## 🔑 Key Insights

- **1,200** total complaints analyzed
- **65.25%** resolution rate | **27.7 hrs** average resolution time
- **Fraud Investigation** cases took ~61 hrs to resolve — nearly 2x the overall average, flagging a clear operational bottleneck
- Complaint volume was fairly evenly distributed across **Branch, Call Center, Email, Mobile App, and Website**
- Average customer satisfaction rating: **3.2 / 5**
- Resolution status split: **65% Resolved | 15% Pending | 20% In Progress**

## 🛠️ Tools & Techniques

- **MS Excel** — PivotTables, Power Query, VLOOKUP, interactive slicers
- Data cleaning & transformation
- KPI reporting & dashboard design
- Root-cause / bottleneck analysis on service resolution data
